Q4: How does browser caching contribute to a faster website experience?

Q5: What role does a Content Delivery Network (CDN) play in web performance?

Enter the global stage with a Content Delivery Network (CDN). CDNs distribute your website's static content across servers worldwide. By serving content from servers closer to users, CDNs reduce latency, minimize load times, and ensure a consistently smooth browsing experience, regardless of geographic location.
