Hospital Management System

Project Overview

Benefits for Users:

The Hospital Management System streamlines operations and improves user experience in healthcare facilities. It offers quick access to patient records, facilitates online appointment scheduling, and enables seamless communication among staff. Efficient inventory management prevents shortages, while accurate billing and financial features ensure transparency. Robust security measures protect patient data, enhancing overall efficiency and patient satisfaction.

Project Details

The Hospital Management System I developed is a web application built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. It efficiently manages patient records, appointments, staff schedules, inventory, and billing. Users can securely access patient information, schedule appointments, and communicate effectively. The system streamlines administrative tasks, reduces wait times, and ensures adequate medical supply levels. It maintains accurate financial records and incorporates security measures to protect sensitive data. Overall, it improves operational efficiency, productivity, and patient satisfaction in healthcare facilities.
