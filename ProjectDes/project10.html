SMART OPEN TERRACE RESTURANT with Blynk App - IoT

Project Overview

The objective of the Smart Roof Control System is to revolutionize the outdoor dining experience by seamlessly integrating IoT technology and intelligent sensors to ensure the comfort, safety, and satisfaction of restaurant guests regardless of weather conditions. This system aims to enhance operational efficiency by providing real-time data on occupancy levels and weather patterns to restaurant owners/managers via a user-friendly mobile application. Key features include automatic adjustment of roof shade based on weather conditions, manual controls for personalized adjustments, and a seamless interface powered by the ESP32 microcontroller. Ultimately, the goal is to eliminate weather-related disruptions and optimize the outdoor dining environment for an unparalleled guest experience.

Project Details

Introducing the Smart Roof Control System: a game-changer for open-air restaurants. It's like having a personal weather guardian for your diners, ensuring they stay comfy and safe no matter what Mother Nature throws their way. Powered by cutting-edge IoT technology and clever sensors, this system keeps an eye on everything from occupancy levels to weather patterns, all in real-time. At its core is the mighty ESP32 microcontroller, connecting all the dots seamlessly. When guests arrive, they're greeted by an infrared sensor that counts them in. This info zips straight to the Blynk app on your phone, giving you a heads-up on how busy things are. Plus, with manual controls right at your fingertips, you can adjust the roof shade to suit your guests' needs, whether it's shielding them from rain or letting the sunshine in. But here's the best part: no more scrambling to protect diners when the weather turns sour. Our system automatically adjusts the roof shade based on weather conditions, thanks to a smart raindrop sensor. That means uninterrupted dining pleasure, come rain or shine. In a nutshell, the Smart Roof Control System isn't just about enhancing the dining experience; it's about boosting operational efficiency and keeping your guests happy and safe. So say goodbye to weather worries and hello to hassle-free outdoor dining.
